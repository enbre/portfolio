About me

As a mechanical designer turned frontend developer, I bring a meticulous approach to every project I work on. Years of experience working with tools — from a plunge router to a computer mouse — and creating custom projects has shown me the importance of joining form and function to build things that are useful and can be enjoyed by anyone.

This is the view I take with programming, too, as I combine my knowledge of design and craftsmanship with technical skills to achieve efficient code solutions that I hope can excite people and improve lives. When not on my computer, I'm usually playing with my two daughters or enjoying the outdoors, especially riding my bike.

Unicorn Detectorclose

A whimsical mobile app that allows you to search for nearby unicorns and see a log of past unicorn sightings.
